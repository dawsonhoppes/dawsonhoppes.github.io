Tltc


The Teaching and Learning Transformation Center's (TLTC) mission is to support and help instructors at the University of Maryland improve and strengthen their teaching framework. With over 5,000 instructors on campus ranging from full time staff to graduate teaching assistants, there are a lot of people the center supports.
How My Team Helped

As part of my advanced usability testing course, my team and I have partnered with the TLTC leadership team to do an investigative study into the center's website. We will help identify problems with the current site, interview stakeholders, brainstorm and implement solutions.

Meet the Client

We presented our problem statement and initial assumptions to our client. Through this meeting we were able to get a better understanding of the scope of the center's impact on the campus and community. This helped us identify key stakeholders, and further develop our research plan.
